ATAP Solar Savings Calculation

Total Solar Generation:
Formula: System Capacity × Peak Sun Hours × 30 days
Example: 9.10 kWp × 3.4 hours × 30 = 900.90 kWh
900.90 kWh
Self-Consumption (Daytime Usage):
Formula: Total Generation × Self-Consumption %
Example: 900.90 kWh × 30% = 270.27 kWh
270.27 kWh (30%)
Battery Storage (Night Usage):
Formula: Stored Solar × 30 days
Example: 8.70 kWh/day × 30 = 261.00 kWh
261.00 kWh
Exported to TNB (ATAP):
Formula: Total Generation - Self-Consumption - Battery Storage
Example: 900.90 kWh - 270.27 kWh - 261.00 kWh = 369.63 kWh
369.63 kWh
Direct Savings from Self-Consumption:
Formula: Self-Consumption × Retail Tariff Rate
Example: 270.27 kWh × RM 0.443/kWh = RM 119.73
RM 119.73
Battery Storage Savings:
Formula: Battery Storage × Retail Tariff Rate
Example: 261.00 kWh × RM 0.443/kWh = RM 115.62
RM 115.62
ATAP Export Credit (SMP Rate):
Formula: Exported Solar × SMP Rate
Example: 369.63 kWh × RM 0.20/kWh = RM 73.93
RM 73.93
Total Savings from Solar (ATAP):
Formula: Direct Savings + Battery Savings + Export Credit
Example: RM 119.73 + RM 115.62 + RM 73.93 = RM 309.28
RM 309.28
Note: In ATAP scheme, exported solar is credited at SMP rate (not used to offset energy, capacity, or network charges)
